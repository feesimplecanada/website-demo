FeeSimple is a specialized legal bill auditing firm. Our mission is simple: to help businesses, entrepreneurs, legal departments, organizations, and charities get transparency and confidence in their legal billing. We also act for law firms who are being unfairly assessed by their clients. Founded by experienced lawyers who understand both sides of the table, we bring deep legal expertise with a collaborative mindset. We work with you and your legal service providers to review billing practices, ensure alignment with fee agreements, and provide recommendations that build stronger, more transparent partnerships.

What We Do

At FeeSimple, we analyze your legal bills – from the first invoice to the last. Our experienced team reviews your dockets line by line and delivers a clear, written report that outlines whether the billing is clear, fair, and reasonable. If we find discrepancies or unjustified charges, we begin a respectful dialogue with your law firm to negotiate a fee reduction based on our findings. If your lawyer refuses to resolve the issue appropriately, and with your direction, we proceed to schedule an assessment. It’s just that simple.

Our Services

1. Legal Invoice Review

We assess legal bills to identify inconsistencies, non-compliant charges, block billing, vague entries, or charges that fall outside of agreed terms.

2. Retainer & Fee Agreement Analysis

We review existing or proposed retainer agreements to ensure that they are clearly defined, enforceable, and aligned with your expectations.

3. Ongoing Billing Oversight

We can provide monthly or quarterly oversight on legal spend to monitor trends, identify red flags, and benchmark firm performance.

4. Custom Reporting

We help you understand your legal spend through easy-to-read reporting – by matter, by firm, by timekeeper, and more.

If you believe you have not been billed fairly by your lawyer, chances are you may be right. FeeSimple will provide you with their opinion for no charge. It’s just that simple!
